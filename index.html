Deskrambler

A Chrome Extension that simplifies LinkedIn by showing only jobs and removing distractions.

Add to Chrome

What it does

Deskrambler removes LinkedIn features that get in your way—like the feed, messages, notifications, and sidebars—so you can focus on the job hunt.

Why it exists

LinkedIn is a valuable job search tool, but it’s filled with distractions. Deskrambler strips it down to the essentials so you can spend less time scrolling and more time landing opportunities.

What it hides

  • The LinkedIn feed
  • Notifications and messaging panels
  • Sidebars and non-job-related widgets

What it keeps

  • Jobs tab and job search functionality
  • Your profile and resume tools
  • Recruiter messages and job applications

Privacy-first by design

Deskrambler does not track you, store any data, or communicate with external servers. It runs locally in your browser and affects only the LinkedIn page you're viewing.
